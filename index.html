Mark Stokes
Eclectic Musician, Performer, Student
Mark Stokes

Mark Stokes latest single Footprint in Me released
recently on itunes, and showcases his eclectic music.

As a multi-instrumentalist, Mark's music is widespread and contrasting at times, and can be most closely associated with indie pop and alternative pop genres.

Mark is a local Georgia musician who performs in Dahlonega, Blue Ridge, Cumming, Alpharetta, Roswell, and Decatur. Mark isn't opposed to travelling further and expanding his horizons.
